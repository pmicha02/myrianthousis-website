About

Myrianthousis Architects Ltd is a Cyprus-based architectural practice established in 1998, with extensive experience delivering projects both in Cyprus and internationally. Founded by lead architect Marios Myrianthousis, the firm provides comprehensive architectural services, from concept design and planning to site supervision and project management.

The practice comprises a team of internationally trained architects and consultants, working across public, residential, commercial, interior, retail, and product design. Its holistic approach ensures that every project is developed with careful attention to context, functionality, sustainability, and detail.

Over the years, Myrianthousis Architects has built a strong network of architects, engineers, and project managers, enabling the formation of specialized teams tailored to each project. This collaborative structure allows the firm to deliver efficient, high-quality solutions that balance design excellence, technical performance, and cost.

Working closely with clients, users, and partners, Myrianthousis Architects creates enduring buildings and spaces that provide long-term value and a sustainable lifecycle.
